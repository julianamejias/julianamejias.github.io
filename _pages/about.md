
About me
======

I am Juliana Mejía Sepúlveda, a Ph.D. student in Environmental Engineering from the University of Arizona, working  with [Dr. Sylvia Sullivan](https://sylviasullivan.github.io/) My research focuses on studying the frequency and duration aerosol extreme events (AEEs) and their main meteorological regimes over the U.S. Southwest.
